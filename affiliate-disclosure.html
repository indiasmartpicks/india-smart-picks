What Is an Affiliate Link?

An affiliate link is a special URL that tracks referrals from our website to a merchant's site. When you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Our Affiliate Relationship

India Smart Picks participates in the Amazon Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by linking to Amazon.in and affiliated sites.

How It Works

  • We curate product recommendations and link them to Amazon India.
  • When you click our links and make a purchase, Amazon pays us a small commission.
  • You pay the same price whether you use our link or go directly to Amazon.

Our Commitment
